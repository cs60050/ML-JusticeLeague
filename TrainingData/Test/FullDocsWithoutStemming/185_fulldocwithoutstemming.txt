Oil prices rebound slightly .
BANGKOK Thailand AP Oil prices rebounded slightly Wednesday in Asia after tumbling more than $ 5 in the previous session as traders worried about the health of the global economy cashed in gains from the recent rally .
Tuesday drop hurled crude back to levels not seen since June 26 .
The market bearish turn this week erases at least for the time being the effect of a that pushed prices past $ 145 a barrel in a string of sessions before the Fourth of July holiday .
Analysts attributed much of the recent to saying traders were cashing in on the previous week gains .
